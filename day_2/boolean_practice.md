## Equality Operators

- == Equals
- != Does not Equal
- > Greater than
- < Less than
- >= Greater than or equal to
- <= Less than or equal to
- <=> Combined comparison


## Boolean examples
- 1 == 3 && 1 == 4 false
- 1 == 3 || 1 == 1 True
- 1 != 3 && 1 != 4 true
- 4 != 3 || 1 != 1 False
